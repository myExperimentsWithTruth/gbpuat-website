Programmes

The College offers programmes at every level of the university.

  • B.V.Sc. & A.H. — the five-year undergraduate veterinary degree
  • M.V.Sc. — across specialisations, admission via Entrance Examination
  • Ph.D. — doctoral research in every department

Teaching hospital

The College operates a full veterinary teaching hospital on campus, which handles both clinical training for students and public animal-health services.
