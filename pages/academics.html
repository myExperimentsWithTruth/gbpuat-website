Admissions

Admission to Pantnagar is through the university's own Entrance Examination for undergraduate and postgraduate programmes, and through ICAR's national exams for a share of postgraduate seats.

The admissions portal is a separate university platform at gbpuat.org.in. Applications, fee payment and status tracking happen there.

Programmes

Undergraduate, master's and doctoral programmes are offered across the eight colleges of the university. The undergraduate degrees include B.Sc. (Hons.) Agriculture, B.V.Sc. and A.H., B.F.Sc., B.Tech (in several branches), B.Sc. Community Science, and B.Sc. Basic Sciences. Master's degrees cover every discipline of each college. Doctoral programmes are offered department-wise across all colleges.

Scholarships and fellowships

Scholarships available at the university include:

  • Merit-based scholarships for top-ranked students.
  • Category scholarships administered under Government of India and state schemes.
  • ICAR Junior and Senior Research Fellowships for eligible postgraduate students.
  • University-specific fellowships instituted by alumni and donors.

Anti-Ragging

Pantnagar operates a zero-tolerance policy on ragging. Details of the Anti-Ragging Committee, the university's squad, the 24×7 helpline, and how to file a complaint are on the dedicated Anti-Ragging page.
