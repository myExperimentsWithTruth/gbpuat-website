The university is a statutory institution established under the Uttarakhand Govind Ballabh Pant University of Agriculture & Technology Act. Its administration is organised around a small number of statutory offices and a wider set of academic and executive posts.

Statutory offices

  • Chancellor — the Governor of Uttarakhand, ex officio. The Chancellor is the head of the university.
  • Vice-Chancellor — the principal executive and academic officer of the university, appointed by the Chancellor.
  • Deans — heads of the eight constituent colleges.
  • Directors — heads of the Directorates of Experiment Station, Extension Education, Students' Welfare, and other functional directorates.
  • Registrar — the chief administrative officer, responsible for university records, examinations, statutory correspondence, and human resource matters.
  • Comptroller — the chief financial officer.
  • University Librarian — head of the Central Library and its systems.

Bodies of governance

Academic and executive decisions are taken through statutory bodies:

  • Board of Management — the executive body of the university.
  • Academic Council — the principal academic authority.
  • Finance Committee — advises on all financial matters.
  • Board of Studies — for each faculty, framing curricula and examination.

Officers under whom the university functions

The public offices whose incumbents serve as Chancellor, patron and minister are held at any given time by the Governor of Uttarakhand, the Chief Minister of the state, and the Minister of Agriculture of the state. Their current holders are shown on the homepage leadership block.

The administrative setup preserves a balance between statutory autonomy of the university and its accountability to the state and to the Government of India through the University Grants Commission and the Indian Council of Agricultural Research.

Where to write

For general correspondence, use the Registrar's office. For academic matters, the office of the relevant Dean or Director. For research matters, the Directorate of Experiment Station. For extension matters, the Directorate of Extension Education. The full contact block is in the footer of every page.
