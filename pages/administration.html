How the university is run

Administrative Setup

The offices, statutory positions, deans, directors and senior officers under whom Govind Ballabh Pant University of Agriculture & Technology functions.

The complete administrative directory of the university — every Dean, Director, Head of Department, Officer, Coordinator and Registrar — is maintained on the live gbpuat.ac.in setup page. That directory changes regularly as officers are appointed, transferred or retire; the authoritative record lives at the source.

For this redesign, the page carries three things: a short guide to how the university is structurally organised, links out to the roles that appear on every visit, and the live source for the complete current list.

Statutory offices

  • Chancellor — the Governor of Uttarakhand, ex officio. Read the Chancellor's message →
  • Vice-Chancellor — the principal executive and academic officer, appointed by the Chancellor. Read the Vice-Chancellor's message →
  • Registrar — the chief administrative officer.
  • Comptroller — the chief financial officer.
  • Deans — heads of the eight constituent colleges.
  • Directors — heads of the functional directorates (Experiment Station, Extension Education, Students' Welfare, and others).
  • University Librarian — head of the Central Library.

Governance bodies

  • Board of Management — the executive body of the university.
  • Academic Council — the principal academic authority.
  • Finance Committee — advises on all financial matters.
  • Board of Studies — one per faculty; frames curricula and examinations.

Deans of the eight colleges

Each of the eight constituent colleges is headed by a Dean appointed by the Vice-Chancellor. To read about each college:

Directorates

Functional directorates that co-ordinate specific mandates across the university:
