The Pantnagar Journal of Research is the university's peer-reviewed research publication. Its scope covers original research and reviews across the agricultural, veterinary, fisheries, community, basic and applied sciences.

Scope

The Journal publishes:

  • Original research from Pantnagar and from other institutions.
  • Reviews on themes of national relevance in agriculture and its allied sciences.
  • Short communications on techniques, observations, and applied findings.

Submissions

The Journal accepts submissions from researchers within the university and outside. Editorial standards follow the norms of peer-reviewed academic publication.

Editorial board

The editorial board is drawn from the university's senior faculty across the constituent colleges, with external reviewers invited on subject-specific basis.
