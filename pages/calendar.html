The university follows a two-semester academic calendar. Semester I typically runs from July to December and Semester II from January to June, with a break between them. Specific dates for the current session are approved by the Academic Council each year.

Structure of the year

  • Semester I — July to December
  • Winter break — end of December
  • Semester II — January to June
  • Summer break — June

Examinations

Each semester carries mid-semester and end-semester examinations. Practical, field and viva-voce components are examined within the departmental timetable. Final results are published by the Registrar's office and displayed in the results section.

Vacations and holidays

Vacations follow the semester breaks. The university observes national and state holidays declared by the Government of India and the Government of Uttarakhand.

Where the current calendar lives

The current academic calendar in the form approved by the Academic Council is available for download from the Registrar's office. Please refer to the source link at the bottom of this page for the latest version.
