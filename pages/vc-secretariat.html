The Vice-Chancellor's Secretariat co-ordinates the office of the Vice-Chancellor — correspondence, appointments, board and council business, meeting schedules and university-wide communications.

What the Secretariat handles

  • Correspondence to and from the Vice-Chancellor
  • Preparation of agenda for the Board of Management and Academic Council
  • Co-ordination with Deans and Directors
  • Ceremonial and official engagements
  • Records of the Vice-Chancellor's office
