The university's Store & Purchase Office runs the tender process for procurement across the colleges, directorates and residential estate. Open tenders are published from time to time.

Where tenders are published

Active tenders are posted on the live university site under the Store & Purchase and Estate Office pages. The Store & Purchase and Estate Office pages carry the current notices.

How to participate

Interested vendors follow the process specified in each tender notice — typically a signed proposal, technical and commercial bid, and earnest money deposit. Timelines and eligibility are as stated in the notice.
