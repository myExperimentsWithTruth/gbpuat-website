The Registrar is the chief administrative officer of the university, appointed by the Board of Management. The office is the point of contact for statutory correspondence, service matters, examinations, and university-wide records.

Functions

  • Custody of the university's official records and the common seal
  • Statutory correspondence and notifications
  • Human resource administration for faculty, officers and staff
  • Conduct of university-wide examinations
  • Service and salary matters
  • Convocation and formal ceremonies
