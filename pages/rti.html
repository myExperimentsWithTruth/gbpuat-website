The Right to Information Act, 2005 gives every citizen the right to obtain information from a public authority. Govind Ballabh Pant University of Agriculture & Technology, as a public university, comes under the Act.

Who to write to

Applications should be addressed to the Public Information Officer of the university, through the Registrar's office. The First Appellate Authority is a senior officer of the university designated for this purpose.

Fees

The prescribed application fee, and additional charges for copying and postage, are as per the Rules under the Act.

Timelines

Applications are responded to within the statutory timeline of thirty days from receipt, or forty-five days in matters involving life and liberty.
