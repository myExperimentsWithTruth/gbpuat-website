Accommodation for visiting faculty, delegations, alumni and official guests of the university.

Booking

Rooms are made available to visiting faculty, official delegations, alumni returning to campus and other institutional guests. Requests are routed through the office of the sponsoring department or the Registrar's office.

Dining

Meals are provided on request; standard breakfast, lunch and dinner services are available in the Guest House dining hall.
