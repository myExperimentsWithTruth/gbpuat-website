The working farm at the heart of a land-grant university — teaching, research and demonstration land, operated as one integrated unit.

What the Farm is

The University Farm is where the practical face of every discipline of the university is enacted. Field trials, crop demonstrations, animal husbandry, fisheries, horticulture and forestry are all conducted on integrated farm land under the direction of the Directorate of Experiment Station.

Use

The Farm is a teaching resource for undergraduate and postgraduate students, a research site for faculty and PhD scholars, and a demonstration platform during Kisan Mela and other public engagement events.
