The university operates a formal grievance redressal mechanism under statutory guidance from the University Grants Commission and the Government of Uttarakhand. Grievances can be raised by students, faculty and staff.

What can be raised

Any complaint relating to academic administration, hostel life, examinations, conduct of staff or students, harassment, discrimination or service matters can be raised through this mechanism.

How to raise a grievance

  1. Write in the first instance to the Head of the department, Warden or immediate supervisor.
  2. If not resolved, address the Grievance Redressal Committee through the Registrar's office.
  3. Statutory matters (sexual harassment, ragging, discrimination) are routed to the dedicated statutory committees whose contacts are published separately.
