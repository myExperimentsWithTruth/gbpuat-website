Mandate

The Centre for Good Governance is the university's institutional programme for governance, transparency and process improvement — working with statutory offices, colleges and directorates to strengthen decision-making, records and accountability.

Functions

  • Institutional review of governance processes
  • Training programmes for staff and officers
  • Support to statutory bodies on process design
  • Documentation of best practices
