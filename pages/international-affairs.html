Mandate

The Directorate of International Affairs handles the university's engagement with foreign universities and institutions — memoranda of understanding, faculty and student exchange, joint research programmes, and hosting international delegations.

Functions

  • MoUs with foreign universities and research institutes
  • Faculty and student exchange programmes
  • Joint research funding and collaboration
  • Hosting of international delegations
  • Study-in-India co-ordination
