Balnilyam is the school on the Pantnagar residential campus — serving the children of faculty, staff and the local community.

About the school

The school offers education from kindergarten through senior secondary, with a curriculum aligned to the state and national board frameworks. It is one of the older institutions on campus, having grown alongside the university itself.

Life on campus

The school is deeply embedded in the university's residential rhythm — its calendar, sports, cultural programme and community events overlap with the university's own.
