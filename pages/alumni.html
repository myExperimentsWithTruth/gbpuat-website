The alumni of Govind Ballabh Pant University of Agriculture & Technology are, in scale and reach, the largest continuing community the institution possesses. Sixty-five years of graduates work today across Indian and international agriculture, research, government, industry, finance, and public life.

The Alumni Alma Mater Advancement Association (4A)

The 4A is the formal alumni association of the university, with the Vice-Chancellor as its ex officio President. Chapters exist across Indian cities and internationally.

Stay connected

Alumni can update their contact details, subscribe to the university's newsletter, and register interest in mentoring, guest lectures, chapter activities and giving-back programmes through the alumni office.

The alumni are what let a public university do what it could not otherwise do — reach places, build tools, and mentor students at a scale no budget line-item alone can support.

Where to write

The alumni office is co-ordinated through the Registrar and the Directorate of Students' Welfare. Formal engagements are hosted through the 4A.
