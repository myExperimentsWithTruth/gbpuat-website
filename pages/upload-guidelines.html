This page describes the process by which departments, offices and directorates should submit material for publication on the university website.

What can be uploaded

  • Official notices, notifications and circulars
  • Announcements of events, workshops, examinations, results
  • Research and academic reports
  • Photographs and video from university events

Approval process

Material must be reviewed and approved by the head of the sponsoring office (Dean, Director, Registrar) before submission. The Directorate of Communication reviews for editorial and technical standards before publication.

Format requirements

PDFs for formal notices, JPEG or PNG for images, MP4 for videos. Files should be checked for sensitive personal information before submission.
