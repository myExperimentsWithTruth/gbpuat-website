Post Graduate Studies is not a college in the departmental sense — it is the umbrella under which master's and doctoral programmes across all seven other colleges are gathered, examined, and awarded.

How PG at Pantnagar works

Every master's and doctoral candidate is admitted through the university-wide Entrance Examination or via ICAR AIEEA (for a share of postgraduate seats). Once admitted, a student is registered simultaneously with the discipline of their host college and with PG Studies for degree, examination and thesis co-ordination.

Programmes

Master's degrees are offered across ten disciplines of the College of Agriculture, all disciplines of the Colleges of Veterinary & Animal Sciences, Technology, Basic Sciences & Humanities, Community Science, Fisheries, and the MBA of the College of Agribusiness Management.

Doctoral programmes are available department-wise across every college.

Fellowships

Eligible postgraduate students are supported by ICAR Junior and Senior Research Fellowships, university-instituted fellowships, and category-specific national schemes.
