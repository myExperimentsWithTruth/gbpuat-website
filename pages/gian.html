GIAN — the Global Initiative of Academic Networks — is a Government of India programme through which Indian universities invite senior foreign faculty to deliver short courses on campus.

At Pantnagar

The GIAN cell at Pantnagar co-ordinates faculty applications for GIAN grants, the conduct of GIAN short courses on campus, and the registration of students and researchers.

Recent courses

The university has hosted GIAN courses across agriculture, veterinary science, technology, food science and community science, taught by faculty from partner universities in the United States, Europe and Australia.
