Mandate

The Directorate of Administration & Monitoring co-ordinates the administrative arm of the university and monitors implementation of statutory and policy decisions across offices, colleges and directorates.

Functions

  • Administrative co-ordination across offices and colleges
  • Monitoring of Board and Council decisions to closure
  • Compliance with statutory and audit requirements
  • Support to the Registrar's office on cross-cutting matters
