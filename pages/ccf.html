The university's shared computing infrastructure and academic software services.

What CCF provides

The Central Computing Facility maintains university-wide computing infrastructure, campus networks, mail services, licensed academic software, and computing time for teaching and research. It supports the Colleges and Directorates in their computational needs.

Services

  • Campus network and Wi-Fi
  • University email services
  • Academic and statistical software
  • Compute time for research projects
