On-campus health services for students, staff and their families — and a training site for the College of Veterinary & Animal Sciences.

Services

The University Hospital provides outpatient consultation, emergency care, laboratory diagnostics, pharmacy and inpatient beds for the university community. Specialist referral to nearby tertiary centres is arranged where required.

Community

Members of the resident university community are covered by the hospital's standing arrangements. Visiting scholars, delegations and Kisan Mela participants can access emergency and basic outpatient services.
