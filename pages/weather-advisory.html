Weather and crop advisory issued by the Directorate of Extension Education, in co-ordination with the university's meteorology, plant-pathology and entomology units.

Purpose

Farmers around Pantnagar and across Uttarakhand rely on the university's crop advisory for time-sensitive decisions — sowing windows, irrigation, pest control, harvest timing. The advisory is issued as a rolling recommendation, reviewed weekly and revised more frequently during critical periods.

Where advisories are issued

Advisories are published through the Directorate of Extension Education page on the live university site, and are also distributed through the Krishi Vigyan Kendras and the local Kisan Sanchar network.
