Pantnagar is a designated centre under the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) — participating in standards development, hosting internships and running public awareness programmes on Indian standards.

What we do

  • Six-month student internships under the BIS programme
  • Contribution to standards development in agricultural machinery, food and materials
  • BIS quizzes, workshops and technical sessions on campus
  • Support to industry on standards compliance

How to participate

Students interested in a BIS internship apply through the BIS notice published each cycle. Faculty interested in standards work write to the BIS coordinator at the university.
