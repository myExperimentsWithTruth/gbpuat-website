The university's organisation follows a clear hierarchy under statutory offices. Every college, directorate and cell reports up through defined lines to the Vice-Chancellor and, ultimately, to the Chancellor.

The apex

  • Chancellor — Governor of Uttarakhand (ex officio)
  • Vice-Chancellor — principal executive and academic officer

Academic wing

Eight colleges, each headed by a Dean; the College of Post Graduate Studies as an umbrella.

Research wing

Directorate of Experiment Station, its research centres and field stations.

Extension wing

Directorate of Extension Education, KVKs, SAMETI, ATIC, Training & Visit Unit.

Executive wing

Registrar's office, Comptroller, Estate, Store & Purchase, Security, University Librarian, University Hospital.

Governance bodies

  • Board of Management
  • Academic Council
  • Finance Committee
  • Board of Studies (per faculty)
